Why Common Praxis?

Praxis is the process by which a theory, lesson, or skill is enacted, embodied, or realized. "Common" refers to both our shared spaces and our shared humanity. This internet web page is where we move from “What is the good life?” to “How do we build a suburb that allows us to live it?”
